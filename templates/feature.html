Our Features!!

Sentiment analysis from web.

Sentiment analysis from web is the one of the features of our website where you could analyze the sentiment of the news from the web. You can analyze the sentiment of the news, articles, etc, from the web just by inserting the link of the news or article.

Text's Sentiment Analysis

Live Sentiment Analysis is another features of our website where you could analyze the sentiment of your text, words, paragrahps, sentences, etc, on the spot. You can also analyze the sentiment of your desired sentences by copy pasting the text in the text box. Positive, negative and neutral result would be shown based on your input.

Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Twitter Sentiment Analysis is also one of the features of our website where you could analyze the sentiment from twitter. You can analyze the sentiment of your tweets, aswell as others tweets if they are public. Positive, negative and neutral result would be shown based on the text in the twitter.
